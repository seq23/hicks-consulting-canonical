About Monika Hicks, LCSW

Quick answer: Monika Hicks leads Hicks Consulting with a grounded, culturally aware approach designed for Black women navigating emotional overload, changing seasons, and the pressure to stay strong no matter what.

Her current site already frames the practice around Solution Focused Therapy, support for women balancing multiple responsibilities, and care that helps women communicate more clearly, set better boundaries, and feel more grounded in daily life.

This version of the site keeps that foundation while elevating the presentation and making the therapy and coaching paths easier to understand.

Hicks Consulting, PLLC

Therapy in Tennessee and coaching nationwide for Black women navigating burnout, boundaries, and life transitions.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are limited to Tennessee. Coaching is not therapy and is not a substitute for mental health treatment.
