Virtual coaching

Coaching for the next version of your life.

Coaching offers non-clinical support for people who want help with boundaries, confidence, burnout recovery, self-trust, decision-making, and life transitions.

It is designed for people who feel ready for change and want a thoughtful space to reflect, get clear, and move forward with more intention.

Coaching can be especially helpful when you are not in crisis, but you do know you want support, structure, and a different way of moving through this season of your life.

What coaching supports

Decision-making, boundaries, and self-trust.

Coaching may help with work-life alignment, identity shifts, leadership pressure, emotional wellness habits, communication patterns, confidence, and the process of choosing differently after years of over-functioning.

Who it helps

For high-functioning women who need room to be honest.

Coaching can be especially helpful for women who look capable on the outside but feel stretched thin internally and want more clarity, steadier self-trust, and more intentional next steps.

Therapy vs Coaching

Therapy supports clinical mental health care for people who want a deeper space to work through emotional pain, stress, patterns, relationships, burnout, or healing that feels hard to navigate alone.

Coaching is non-clinical support for people who want clarity, accountability, reflection, and practical help as they move through transitions, decision-making, boundaries, confidence, or next steps.

Therapy

Clinical mental health support

  • Deeper emotional healing and patterns
  • Support for stress, burnout, relationships, and mental health care
  • For people who want a therapeutic space to process what feels hard to carry alone
Coaching

Non-clinical support

  • Clarity, accountability, and practical next steps
  • Support for transitions, confidence, boundaries, and intentional change
  • For people who feel ready to move differently and want structure along the way

Both paths offer support, but they serve different needs. If you are unsure where to begin, start with the secure booking link and choose the option that feels closest to your current need.

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps our clients heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, consulting, and mental health support.

Through therapy, coaching, consulting, groups, and organizational training, Hicks Consulting supports clients of all ethnicities while bringing specialized care for Black women to the center of the practice.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are virtual and limited to Tennessee. Coaching is not therapy and is not a substitute for mental health treatment.
