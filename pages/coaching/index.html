Virtual coaching

Coaching for the next version of your life.

Virtual coaching at Hicks Consulting is non-clinical support for women who want help with boundaries, confidence, burnout recovery, self-trust, transitions, and intentional living.

Coaching is not therapy. It is a structured support space for people who want clarity, accountability, and practical reflection as they choose a healthier way to live.

Book Coaching
What coaching supports

Decision-making, boundaries, and self-trust.

Coaching may help with work-life alignment, identity shifts, leadership pressure, emotional wellness habits, communication patterns, confidence, and the process of choosing differently after years of over-functioning.

Who it helps

For high-functioning women who need room to be honest.

High-functioning, overwhelmed women—especially professionals, mothers, and faith-centered individuals—who are tired of just “holding it together” and are ready to heal emotionally, break unhealthy patterns, and create a more intentional, balanced life.

Therapy vs coaching

Therapy addresses clinical mental health care within Tennessee. Coaching is non-clinical support for clarity, growth, and life direction.

If you are unsure which path fits, you can start with the secure booking link and choose the service that matches your current need.
