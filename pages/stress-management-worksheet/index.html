What is inside

One practical reflection page, not another overwhelming guide.

The worksheet is built around a simple four-part stress-management formula: Identify, Eliminate, Minimize, and Manage. It is meant to help you pause, sort, and choose a next step with less mental clutter.

This is an educational resource, not therapy, diagnosis, emergency support, or a substitute for care from a licensed professional.

01

Identify

Write down what is causing stress without judging whether it is big enough.

02

Eliminate

Notice what is not necessary and can be removed.

03

Minimize

Look for what can be reduced, simplified, outsourced, or scheduled differently.

04

Manage

Name the stressors that remain and choose support, care, or structure around them.

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps our clients heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, consulting, and mental health support.

Through therapy, coaching, consulting, groups, and organizational training, Hicks Consulting supports clients of all ethnicities while bringing specialized care for Black women to the center of the practice.
