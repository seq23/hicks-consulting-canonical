Organizational training

In-person mental health trainings for organizations that want healthier people systems.

Hicks Consulting provides in-person trainings for organizations on mental health awareness, emotional intelligence, burnout, compassion fatigue, self-care, boundaries, and supportive workplace culture.

Monika Hicks is not only a therapist. She is a mental health consultant who brings clinical grounding, practical tools, and culturally aware facilitation into rooms where people need better ways to work, lead, communicate, and recover.

Training focus

Practical mental health education for real workplaces.

These sessions are designed for organizations that want more than a motivational talk. The goal is to help people recognize stress patterns, understand emotional wellness, and practice healthier ways to relate to themselves and each other.

Mental health awareness

Build shared language around emotional wellness, stress, support, and how mental health shows up at work.

Burnout and compassion fatigue

Support teams in care-heavy, leadership-heavy, or emotionally demanding environments.

Boundaries and self-care

Move self-care from a slogan into practical skills for sustainability and healthier communication.

Emotional intelligence

Help teams increase self-awareness, empathy, regulation, and more thoughtful conflict navigation.

Who this is for

Teams, leaders, and institutions that need grounded support.

Trainings can support workplaces, nonprofits, schools, healthcare-adjacent teams, women-centered organizations, faith-connected spaces, and staff groups carrying high emotional loads. The audience may include both men and women; the work is rooted in practical mental health education, not one-on-one therapy.

What to expect

Calm facilitation with useful takeaways.

Organizations can expect a thoughtful session that respects the room, avoids shame-based messaging, and gives people language and tools they can actually use. Trainings can be adapted for staff development, wellness programming, leadership conversations, and special events.

Inquiry process

For organizational trainings, email Hicks Consulting directly with the audience, topic, preferred date range, and whether the session will be in person or virtual.
