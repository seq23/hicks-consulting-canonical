Organizational training

In-person mental health trainings and keynote speaking for organizations that want healthier people systems.

Hicks Consulting offers in-person trainings and keynote speaking for organizations that want practical, thoughtful support around mental health, emotional wellness, burnout, boundaries, and healthier workplace culture.

Monika Hicks brings both clinical insight and real-world practicality to conversations about how people work, lead, communicate, and care for themselves and each other.

Training focus

Practical mental health education for real workplaces.

These sessions are designed for organizations looking for more than inspiration alone. The focus is on helping people better recognize stress, strengthen emotional awareness, and build healthier ways of relating at work.

Mental health awareness

Build shared language around emotional wellness, stress, support, and how mental health shows up at work.

Burnout and compassion fatigue

Support teams in care-heavy, leadership-heavy, or emotionally demanding environments.

Boundaries and self-care

Move self-care from a slogan into practical skills for sustainability and healthier communication.

Emotional intelligence

Help teams increase self-awareness, empathy, regulation, and more thoughtful conflict navigation.

Who this is for

Teams, leaders, and institutions that need grounded support.

Trainings can support workplaces, nonprofits, schools, healthcare-adjacent teams, women-centered organizations, faith-connected spaces, and staff groups carrying high emotional loads. The audience may include both men and women; the work is rooted in practical mental health education, not one-on-one therapy.

What to expect

Calm facilitation with useful takeaways.

Organizations can expect a thoughtful session that respects the room, avoids shame-based messaging, and gives people language and tools they can actually use. Trainings can be adapted for staff development, wellness programming, leadership conversations, and special events.

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps our clients heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, consulting, and mental health support.

Through therapy, coaching, consulting, groups, and organizational training, Hicks Consulting supports clients of all ethnicities while bringing specialized care for Black women to the center of the practice.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are virtual and limited to Tennessee. Coaching is not therapy and is not a substitute for mental health treatment.
