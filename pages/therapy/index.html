Therapy in Tennessee

Therapy for Black women who are carrying too much for too long.

Quick answer: Therapy may be the right fit if you want licensed mental health support for emotional overload, anxiety, depression-related heaviness, boundary strain, relationship stress, or life transitions while living in Tennessee.

This practice is designed for Black women who are used to functioning through pressure. Sometimes that looks like burnout. Sometimes it looks like snapping, shutting down, overthinking, or feeling numb in a life that seems perfectly fine from the outside.

What support can look like here

  • In-person therapy in Memphis
  • Telehealth therapy across Tennessee
  • Support around stress, burnout, overwhelm, boundaries, self-worth, transitions, relationship stress, and emotional heaviness
