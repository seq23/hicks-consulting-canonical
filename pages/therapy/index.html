Virtual therapy

Therapy for women who are ready to stop surviving their own life.

Therapy offers a supportive space to work through burnout, boundaries, stress, life transitions, and emotional healing.

This work may feel especially meaningful for people who are used to holding everything together and are ready for a more honest, intentional way of living, including many women who have spent a long time carrying more than they should.

What therapy can support

When high functioning starts costing too much.

Therapy may support emotional overload, burnout, depression-related heaviness, anxiety-related patterns, self-esteem, boundaries, relationship strain, grief, life transitions, and the exhaustion of being the dependable one in every room.

Virtual format

Care is online only.

Individual counseling through Hicks Consulting is virtual. This keeps the therapy path clear, private, and accessible for clients located in Tennessee.

Fit

This may be a fit if you are ready to name what is no longer working.

Therapy is not about proving how strong you are. It is a place to slow down, listen more closely, and understand what your emotions, patterns, relationships, and body may be asking you to face.

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps our clients heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, consulting, and mental health support.

Through therapy, coaching, consulting, groups, and organizational training, Hicks Consulting supports clients of all ethnicities while bringing specialized care for Black women to the center of the practice.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are virtual and limited to Tennessee. Coaching is not therapy and is not a substitute for mental health treatment.
