Virtual support groups

Guided group support for people who do not want to heal in isolation.

Virtual support groups offer a guided space to reflect, connect, and grow with others who may be carrying similar experiences.

These groups are more than casual conversation. They are intentionally led spaces where participants can feel supported, hear different perspectives, practice new tools, and feel less alone.

What group support is

Connection with structure.

A group session brings multiple people into a guided support setting. The facilitator helps hold the conversation, protect the tone of the space, and keep the group focused on emotional insight, skill-building, and mutual respect.

Why people choose groups

Sometimes healing needs witnesses.

Group support can help people feel less isolated, recognize shared patterns, gain perspective, and practice being honest in a space where other people are also doing meaningful emotional work.

Fit

You may be a fit for a group if...

Group support can be especially helpful for women who feel overwhelmed, overextended, in transition, or in need of both community and professional guidance.

You want support but do not want to feel alone in the processYou benefit from hearing how other people name similar strugglesYou want a guided space, not an unstructured forumYou are willing to participate respectfully and protect confidentiality
Group vs individual support

Different formats support different needs.

Individual therapy gives one-on-one clinical attention. Groups offer shared reflection, community, perspective, and a chance to practice connection in real time. Some people use both formats at different seasons.

What to expect

Virtual, guided, and respectful.

Groups may include topic discussion, reflection prompts, shared experiences, practical tools, and space to notice patterns. Not every group is right for every person, so interest and fit are handled thoughtfully.

Group questions

Are groups therapy?

Some groups may be therapeutic or therapist-led, but the format and purpose depend on the specific group. Hicks Consulting will clarify the structure before enrollment.

Are groups virtual?

Yes. Group support through Hicks Consulting is positioned as virtual unless a specific organization separately arranges an in-person training.

How do I learn about upcoming groups?

Email Hicks Consulting to express interest and ask about current or upcoming group options.

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps our clients heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, consulting, and mental health support.

Through therapy, coaching, consulting, groups, and organizational training, Hicks Consulting supports clients of all ethnicities while bringing specialized care for Black women to the center of the practice.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are virtual and limited to Tennessee. Coaching is not therapy and is not a substitute for mental health treatment.
