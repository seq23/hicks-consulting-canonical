Intake Quiz

Find your best next step.

This ten-question quiz is a self-reflection tool, not a diagnosis. It helps you sort whether therapy, coaching, or both may be the right next step based on what feels most pressing right now.
1. Which feels most true lately?
2. How long has this been building?
3. What keeps showing up?
4. Are you mostly looking for...
5. Does this affect relationships, sleep, focus, or daily functioning?
6. Where are you located?
7. What support area feels closest?
8. Are you mainly trying to recover, process, and feel steadier?
9. Does the Strong Black Woman role feel heavy right now?
10. What kind of next step feels safest?

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps our clients heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, consulting, and mental health support.

Through therapy, coaching, consulting, groups, and organizational training, Hicks Consulting supports clients of all ethnicities while bringing specialized care for Black women to the center of the practice.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are virtual and limited to Tennessee. Coaching is not therapy and is not a substitute for mental health treatment.
