Intake Quiz

Find your best next step.

The short version This ten-question quiz is a self-reflection tool, not a diagnosis. It helps you sort whether therapy, coaching, or both may be the right next step based on what feels most pressing right now.
1. Which feels most true lately?
2. How long has this been building?
3. What keeps showing up?
4. Are you mostly looking for...
5. Does this affect relationships, sleep, focus, or daily functioning?
6. Where are you located?
7. What support area feels closest?
8. Are you mainly trying to recover, process, and feel steadier?
9. Does the Strong Black Woman role feel heavy right now?
10. What kind of next step feels safest?
