Hicks Consulting serves two distinct audiences through one grounded mental health brand: private support for individuals and professional training for teams, leaders, and institutions.

For Individuals Seeking Healing & Support

Therapy and coaching for women who are tired of holding everything together.

If you are high-functioning on the outside but exhausted, anxious, overextended, or disconnected from your own needs, Hicks Consulting offers a grounded space to slow down, feel supported, and rebuild from the inside out.

This pathway supports women navigating burnout, anxiety, people-pleasing, emotional exhaustion, boundaries, and the pressure to keep performing while quietly falling apart.

Individual support

You do not have to keep surviving at the expense of yourself.

For high-achieving women, burnout can look like competence, over-responsibility, perfectionism, people-pleasing, and silently carrying more than anyone realizes.

Hicks Consulting offers therapy and coaching support for women who are ready to stop abandoning themselves, rebuild emotional steadiness, strengthen boundaries, and create a life that is not powered by exhaustion.

The work is warm, direct, and grounded. It is for the woman who has been functioning for everyone else and is ready to feel more present, supported, and whole in her own life. When clinically necessary, Hicks Consulting can also provide referrals for medication management support.

Organizational training

Workforce mental health is no longer a wellness extra. It is a stability issue.

Hicks Consulting helps organizations address burnout, compassion fatigue, trauma exposure, boundaries, emotional sustainability, and healthier workplace culture through strategic trainings and keynote speaking.

When staff are overwhelmed, emotionally depleted, or unsupported, organizations feel it through turnover, disengagement, conflict, absenteeism, and declining trust. Mental health education gives teams shared language, practical tools, and a more sustainable way to work together.

Trainings are designed for real-world institutions that need more than inspiration. They support workforce stability, trauma-informed systems, employee wellbeing, leadership awareness, and healthier people operations.

Organizational expertise

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps our clients heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, consulting, and mental health support.

Through therapy, coaching, consulting, groups, and organizational training, Hicks Consulting supports clients of all ethnicities while bringing specialized care for Black women to the center of the practice.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are virtual and limited to Tennessee. Coaching is not therapy and is not a substitute for mental health treatment.
