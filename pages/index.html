Who this is for

Support for the woman who is done just holding it together.

High-functioning, overwhelmed women—especially professionals, mothers, and faith-centered individuals—who are tired of just “holding it together” and are ready to heal emotionally, break unhealthy patterns, and create a more intentional, balanced life.

Black women are the heart of this practice. Monika also serves clients of all ethnicities and brings her mental health consulting lens into groups, coaching, and larger organizational settings.

Not just therapy

A broader mental health consulting practice.

Monika Hicks is a therapist and mental health consultant who supports individuals, groups, and organizations through virtual care, emotional wellness education, and in-person trainings.
