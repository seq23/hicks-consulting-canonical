The resources are organized by content type, not confusing publishing cadence. Start with shorter insights when you need clarity, articles when you want education, guides when you want structure, and white papers when you want deeper clinical framing.

Hicks Consulting

Hicks Consulting helps Black women heal beyond survival, break unhealthy cycles, and build healthier, more fulfilling lives through virtual therapy, coaching, and mental health support.

This website is for informational purposes only and does not provide emergency or crisis services. Therapy services are virtual and limited to Tennessee. Coaching, consulting, trainings, and groups are not substitutes for emergency care or individualized medical advice.
