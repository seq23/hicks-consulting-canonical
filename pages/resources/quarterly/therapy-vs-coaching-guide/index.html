White Paper

A practical guide to therapy vs coaching for high-achieving Black women

A long-form clinical white paper on the difference between therapy, coaching, and mental health consulting support.

This white paper is designed for people who know they need support but are unsure which kind of support fits the season they are in. Therapy and coaching can both be valuable, but they do not serve the same function.

Therapy

Therapy is licensed mental health care. It can address symptoms, patterns, trauma responses, depression-related heaviness, anxiety-related cycles, grief, relational pain, and emotional distress. Therapy is appropriate when the issue needs clinical assessment, ongoing mental health treatment, or support within a regulated professional relationship.

Coaching

Coaching is non-clinical support. It can help with clarity, accountability, boundaries, transitions, self-trust, professional identity, leadership, and intentional life design. Coaching works best when the person is stable enough to focus on direction, behavior, and implementation.

Mental health consulting

Mental health consulting can sit between education and implementation. For organizations, consulting and training may help teams understand burnout, compassion fatigue, emotional intelligence, supportive culture, and healthier communication. It does not replace therapy for individual employees.

How to decide

If your concern involves symptoms, clinical distress, trauma, safety, or mental health treatment, therapy is the safer starting point. If your concern is direction, accountability, decision-making, or a life transition without clinical urgency, coaching may be appropriate. If your organization needs education or staff support around emotional wellness, training or consulting is the right lane.

Why the distinction matters

Clear boundaries protect the client, the practitioner, and the work. The right format lets support be both ethical and effective. Hicks Consulting keeps these lanes distinct so people can choose the kind of help that fits the actual need.
